Our mission

Built with security and transparency in mind

GhostShare uses end-to-end encryption, removes central data silos, and stays completely free forever. We're community driven, building with and for the people who rely on private conversations every day.

No middlemen

No central servers mean no single point of failure or surveillance.

Open-source transparency

Every line of code is open for review, forks, and improvements.

Community stewardship

The GhostShare community drives the roadmap, features, and audits.

Why choose GhostShare?

Privacy-first by design

Nothing to purchase, nothing to trade away. Just honest communication tooling.

End-to-end encryption

Messages are sealed from the moment they leave your device until they reach the recipient.

Completely free forever

Privacy isn't upsold. GhostShare has no premium tiers or dark patterns.

No data collection

We do not store metadata, contact books, or usage analytics. Ever.

Open-source transparency

Inspect, audit, or contribute. Every decision is visible in the repository.

No central servers

Peer-to-peer architecture keeps conversations resilient and censorship-resistant.

Community driven

Roadmaps, feature votes, and reviews happen in the open with the community.
